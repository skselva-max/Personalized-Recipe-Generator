About Recipe Generator

Recipe Generator is an AI-powered application that helps you create delicious recipes based on the ingredients you have available. Whether you're looking to reduce food waste, get creative with what's in your pantry, or just need inspiration for your next meal, our tool makes cooking easier and more enjoyable.

How It Works

  1. Enter the ingredients you have (separated by commas)
  2. Specify your preferences (servings, difficulty, cuisine type, etc.)
  3. Our AI generates a complete recipe with detailed instructions
  4. Save your favorite recipes to access them later
